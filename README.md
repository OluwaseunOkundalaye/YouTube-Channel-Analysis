## KEY OBSERVATIONS AND RECOMMENDATIONS FOR GROWTH

### Key Observations:

**Subscriber & Engagement Overview:**
- Total Subscribers: 48.1K
- Total Likes: 52,541
- Total Views: 1,020,262
- Total Comments: 7,494
- Total Videos: 43
- Average Views per Video: 23,727
- Average Likes per Video: 1,222
- Average Comments per Video: 174
- No dislikes or shares recorded.

**Most Engaging Videos:**
- Video 3 had the highest engagement across likes (13,000), views (250,923), and comments (607).
- Other high-performing videos include Video 4, 6, 9, and 18.

**Least Engaging Videos:**
- Videos 6, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 15, 16, 17, and 24 had zero likes, showing no engagement.
- Least viewed videos include Video 40 (2,957 views) and Videos 31, 33, 34 with below 6,000 views.

**Engagement Trends Over Time:**
- Likes per Day: Peaks on Wednesdays (19,409 likes) and Fridays (15,294 likes) but significantly drops on Saturdays (541 likes).
- Likes per Month: Highest in August (22,469 likes) but drops sharply in October (1,828 likes).
- Views per Month: Highest in July & August but declines significantly in September and October.
- Views per Year: Increased from 282,895 (2023) to 737,367 (2024), showing strong channel growth.

**Comment Trends:**
- Comments per day peak on Wednesdays (2,484 comments) and drop on Saturdays (93 comments).
- Comments per month decreased from July (2,358) to October (267) but slightly recovered in December (1,404).

### Recommendations for Growth:

**Leverage High-Performing Days for Content Uploads:**
- Since Wednesdays and Fridays have the highest engagement, schedule new video uploads and promotions on these days.
- Saturdays have low engagement, so avoid major content releases.

**Increase Engagement on Underperforming Videos:**
- Identify why some videos have zero likes and consider repackaging or re-promoting them.
- Improve thumbnails, titles, and descriptions to attract more views.
- Encourage calls-to-action (CTAs) to boost likes, shares, and comments.

**Boost Monthly Engagement During Low-Performance Months:**
- September and October saw a decline in likes, views, and comments.
- Run giveaways, Q&A sessions, or trending content to regain audience engagement.

**Optimize Video Content Strategy:**
- The most engaging videos (3, 4, 6, 9, 18) should be analyzed for content patterns.
- Produce more content similar to these videos (topics, style, length, or presentation).

**Encourage More Shares and Community Engagement:**
- No recorded shares suggest a lack of strong community-driven engagement.
- Promote collaborations, challenges, or hashtags to drive shares.
- Ask viewers to share videos in social media groups.

**Capitalize on Yearly Growth Trends:**
- Views increased from 2023 to 2024, indicating potential for further expansion.
- Keep producing consistent, quality content to maintain audience growth.

By applying these insights, the channel can increase engagement, boost visibility, and grow its audience base effectively.
